ECM Records - Touchstones 2019

“One of the defining sound-worlds of the past half-century of recorded music is the distinguished repertory of ECM Records, founded by Manfred Eicher in 1969.” Richard Brody, The New Yorker. Since 1969 ECM stands for pioneering and continuous music production, an anniversary which prompts reflections upon the journey so far. The Touchstones series of reissues highlights albums made along the way and includes many recordings which now count as milestones in the history of jazz and improvisation.
